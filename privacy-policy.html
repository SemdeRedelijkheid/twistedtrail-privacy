Privacy Policy

Game: Twisted Trail
Developer: Camera Crew Games
Type: Downloadable Single-Player Game

Simple Summary

Twisted Trail does not collect, store, transmit, or process any personal data whatsoever. This is a simple downloadable game that you play offline on your device. We have no servers, no accounts, no tracking, and no data collection of any kind.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

Twisted Trail is designed with privacy in mind. We do not collect any information from our users, including but not limited to:

  • Personal identification information (names, email addresses, phone numbers)
  • Device information or specifications
  • Gameplay data or statistics
  • Location data
  • Usage analytics or telemetry
  • Crash reports or error logs
  • Any other form of user data

2. No Online Features

Twisted Trail is a completely offline game. It does not:

  • Connect to the internet
  • Require user accounts or registration
  • Include multiplayer features
  • Send data to external servers
  • Include social media integration
  • Display advertisements

3. Local Data Storage

The game may store the following data locally on your device only:

  • Game settings and preferences
  • Save game progress
  • Configuration files necessary for game operation

This data never leaves your device and is only used for game functionality. You can delete this data by uninstalling the game.

4. Third-Party Services

Twisted Trail does not integrate with any third-party services, analytics platforms, advertising networks, or social media platforms. The game operates independently without external dependencies.

5. Children's Privacy

Since we do not collect any data from anyone, this policy applies to users of all ages, including children under 13. Parents can be confident that no personal information about their children is collected, stored, or transmitted by our game.

6. Changes to This Policy

Since Twisted Trail does not collect any data, this privacy policy is unlikely to change. However, if we ever decide to add features that involve data collection in future versions, we will:

  • Update this privacy policy
  • Clearly communicate any changes
  • Ensure any data collection is transparent and consensual

7. Your Rights

Since we do not collect or store any of your data, there is no personal information for you to access, modify, or delete. You maintain complete control over your gaming experience.

8. Security

While we implement standard security practices in our game development, the best security measure is our commitment to not collecting any data in the first place. What doesn't exist cannot be compromised.

This privacy policy complies with major privacy regulations including:

  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)
  • California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA)
  • Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA)

Compliance is straightforward since we do not collect any personal data.
